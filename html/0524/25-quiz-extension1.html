Menu examples

A menu with navigation links

  • Home
  • About Us
  • Products

VoiceOver will announce the above as links, e.g. "Home, link".

A menu with role=menu

  • Home
  • About Us
  • Products

In the role=menu example, VoiceOver on Mac/iOS will announce "Home, menu item" and after a pause "You are currently on a menu item [...] To close this menu, press Escape".
